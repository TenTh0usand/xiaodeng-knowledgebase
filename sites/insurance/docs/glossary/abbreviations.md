# Common Abbreviations

These abbreviations often appear in insurance emails, reports and system fields.

| Abbreviation | Full Form | 中文含义 | Typical Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| AML | Anti-Money Laundering | 反洗钱 | Compliance checks and transaction monitoring. |
| ALM | Asset-Liability Management | 资产负债管理 | Investment and solvency management. |
| API | Application Programming Interface | 应用程序接口 | Digital insurance systems. |
| APS | Attending Physician Statement | 主治医生报告 | Underwriting and claims evidence. |
| CDD | Customer Due Diligence | 客户尽职调查 | KYC and AML process. |
| CIRC | China Insurance Regulatory Commission | 原中国保监会 | Historical regulatory references. |
| CBIRC | China Banking and Insurance Regulatory Commission | 原银保监会 | Historical regulatory references. |
| NFRA | National Financial Regulatory Administration | 国家金融监督管理总局 | Current China financial regulation context. |
| CRM | Customer Relationship Management | 客户关系管理 | Sales and service systems. |
| CSM | Contractual Service Margin | 合同服务边际 | IFRS 17 financial reporting. |
| FYP | First-Year Premium | 首年保费 | Sales performance reports. |
| APE | Annualized Premium Equivalent | 年化标准保费 | New business reporting. |
| VNB | Value of New Business | 新业务价值 | Life insurer value reporting. |
| EV | Embedded Value | 内含价值 | Life insurer valuation. |
| NBV | New Business Value | 新业务价值 | Alternative abbreviation for VNB. |
| KPI | Key Performance Indicator | 关键绩效指标 | Performance management. |
| KYC | Know Your Customer | 了解你的客户 | Customer onboarding and compliance. |
| LTC | Long-Term Care | 长期护理 | Care insurance and elderly services. |
| TPD | Total Permanent Disability | 全残 | Life and accident insurance benefits. |
| CI | Critical Illness | 重大疾病 | Product names and claim types. |
| ADL | Activities of Daily Living | 日常生活活动能力 | Long-term care assessment. |
| ICU | Intensive Care Unit | 重症监护室 | Medical claim documents. |
| IBNR | Incurred But Not Reported | 已发生未报告 | Claim reserves. |
| RBC | Risk-Based Capital | 风险资本 | Solvency framework. |
| SLA | Service Level Agreement | 服务时效承诺 | Operations and customer service. |
| TAT | Turnaround Time | 处理时效 | Underwriting, claims and servicing reports. |
| POS | Policy Owner Service | 保单持有人服务 | Policy servicing in some insurers. |
| UW | Underwriting | 核保 | New business and risk assessment. |
| NB | New Business | 新业务 | Sales and policy issuance. |
| IF | In Force | 有效保单 | Portfolio status and valuation. |
| SURREN | Surrender | 退保 | System codes and reports. |
| ROP | Return of Premium | 返还保费 | Product features and benefit wording. |

## English Email Examples

- Please check whether the policy is **IF** before accepting the claim.
- The case requires an **APS** because the medical history is unclear.
- The monthly report should include **FYP**, **APE**, **VNB** and persistency.
- The customer passed **KYC** and **AML** screening.
